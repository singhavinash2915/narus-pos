FEATURES

Everything Your Restaurant Needs

A complete POS and management platform designed specifically for NARU's Biryani & Kababs. Fast, intuitive, and built for busy kitchens.

Fast POS Billing

Touch-optimized menu grid with category filters. Add items, pick Half/Full portions, apply discounts and coupons — all in seconds.

Real-time Sync

All billing counters stay in sync instantly. Create an order on Counter 1, see it appear on Counter 2 in real-time. Powered by Supabase.

Multi-Payment Support

Accept Cash, Card, and UPI payments. Auto-calculates change for cash payments. Clean payment flow for quick checkout.

Owner Dashboard

Track revenue, orders, top-selling items, and payment methods. Compare daily, weekly, monthly, and quarterly performance.

Role-Based Access

Staff log in with a quick PIN for billing. Owner logs in with email/password for full admin access. Manage, Dashboard are owner-only.

iOS & Android Apps

Same beautiful app as a native mobile experience. Install on tablets for counter billing and on phone for owner monitoring on the go.

KOT & Print

Generate Kitchen Order Tickets instantly. Print-ready format for thermal printers. Send orders to kitchen with one tap.

Discounts & Coupons

Apply percentage or fixed discounts. Create coupon codes (NARU10, DROOL20) with min order and expiry. BOGO support.

Menu Management

Add, edit, delete menu items and categories. Set Half/Full prices, mark Veg/Non-Veg, toggle availability. Changes sync instantly.
